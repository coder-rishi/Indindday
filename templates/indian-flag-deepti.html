The meaning of Indian flag


Meaning of the three colours in the Indian Flag

Meaning of saffron colour
Saffron, the first band in the Indian flag represents strength and courage of the country.
Meaning of white colour
White, the band in the middle of the Indian flag represents peace and truth. It holds the ashok chakra in the middle.
Meaning of green colour
Green, the last band in the Indian flag represents growth, fertility and auspiciousness of our nation.
